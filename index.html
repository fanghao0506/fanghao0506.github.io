最近工作

Optimized Feature Extraction and Channel Selection for fNIRS-based Online Binary Classification BCIs: A Pearson Correlation and Machine Learning Approach

ongoing

Hao Fang, Yuxin Li

In our study, we optimized fNIRS-based Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) devices for enhanced subject-independent accuracy in binary classification. Using adjacency matrices and tailored machine learning techniques, we strategically positioned transmitters and receivers, achieving a significant high accuracy boost over traditional methods. Our approach streamlines hardware placement and reduces unnecessary channels, setting a new standard for fNIRS-based BCI systems.

Enabling Transcranial Electrical Stimulation via STI01: Experimental Simulations and Hardware Circuit Implementation

EEI 2023

Guanjie Xu, Gaomin Su, Hao Fang, Yue Li

A novel utilization of a chip, STI01, developed in China and traditionally used for muscle stimulation, to meet the demands of transcranial electrical stimulation (tES).
